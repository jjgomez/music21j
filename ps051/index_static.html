zachara.mit.edu?

Antonio Zachara da Teramo (often spelled "Zacara" today) was a composer active around 1400 whose strange but beautiful compositions captivated all of Europe for 40 years. The name of the server is dedicated to the notion that we can all create amazing music if we're willing to step a bit out of our comfort zones. See this link for some music
